Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2025

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you're not satisfied, we'll refund your money. Full stop.

How It Works

The Promise

Within 30 days of purchase, you can request a full refund of your money. You don't need to explain why. You don't need to justify it. You don't need a reason that sounds good enough.

The Process

  1. Send an email to support@airbnb-activation.com
  2. Include your purchase email address
  3. Write "Refund Request" in the subject line
  4. We'll process it within 5 business days

Where the Money Goes

  • If you paid by card: Refund goes back to your card (3-5 business days for bank to show it)
  • If you paid by PayPal: Refund goes back to your PayPal account (same day, usually)
  • If you paid by bank transfer: Refund goes back to your bank account

The Window

30 days from your purchase date. That's it. No tricks with "business days" or fine print. Calendar days. 30 of them.

After 30 days: We can't process a refund (payment processor rules), but we'll still help you. Email and we'll figure something out.

The Fine Print (Actually Honest)

Look, we trust you. But to be transparent about why a refund policy exists:

  • We can't refund if your payment was disputed/charged-back elsewhere (fraud prevention)
  • We can only refund to the original payment method
  • If you purchased resale access (shared account), no refund—that's against our terms

What This Isn't

This isn't a money-back guarantee based on results. We can't control:

  • How much you earn (depends on your effort, location, market)
  • Whether you'll get bookings (depends on your listing, competition)
  • Local laws or regulations in your area
  • Airbnb changing their algorithm or policies

It IS a "we stand behind our product" guarantee. If you buy it and decide it's not for you—refund, no questions.

Why We Do This

Two reasons:

  1. We believe in what we've built. We're confident enough to bet money on it. If you're not happy, that's on us, not you.
  2. We're the opposite of a scam. Real companies stand behind their products. Scams hide. We document. We promise. We deliver.

Questions About a Refund?

Email us. We'll process it fast. No gatekeeping.
