Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

The Short Version

We collect minimal data. We don't sell it. We don't spam. We keep it safe. That's it.

What Data We Collect

When You Buy

  • Email address (to send you the kit and updates)
  • Name (optional, for personalization)
  • Payment info (processed by Stripe; we never see your card)

When You Contact Us

  • Your email address and message content
  • Any files or info you voluntarily share (like your results/success story)

Automatic Data

  • Basic analytics (page views, referrer source — no personal identifiers)
  • Browser type and device (for optimization)
  • Approximate location (country-level only, from IP; no tracking)

What We DON'T Do

  • ❌ Sell your data to third parties
  • ❌ Share your email with marketing companies
  • ❌ Use third-party tracking pixels (like Facebook Pixel)
  • ❌ Profile or segment you for ads
  • ❌ Email you more than 2x per week (usually less)
  • ❌ Store your payment card or sensitive financial data

How We Use Your Data

Data Why
Email Deliver your kit and support emails
Name Personalize welcome emails
Analytics Understand what content helps (improve the kit)
Message content Reply to you and (with permission) improve our FAQ

Security

Your data is stored with encryption. We use industry-standard practices (HTTPS, secure servers). Your payment is never stored—only processed by Stripe (PCI-DSS compliant).

Cookies

We use minimal cookies:

  • Session cookie: Keeps you logged in (if you have an account)
  • Language preference: Remembers your language choice
  • Analytics: Anonymous visitor counting (no personal data)

You can disable cookies in your browser. The site will still work (you just choose your language each time).

Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

If you're in the EU or California, you have rights:

  • Access: See what data we have on you
  • Delete: Ask us to erase your data
  • Correction: Update incorrect info
  • Opt-out: Stop receiving marketing emails

Email us to exercise any of these rights. We'll respond within 30 days.

Third Parties

We use these services (they also have privacy policies):

  • Stripe: Payment processing (they don't share your data with us)
  • Email service: Mailchimp/SendGrid (encrypted, secure)
  • Hosting: Cloudflare (DDoS protection, no logging)
  • Analytics: Plausible or similar (privacy-first, no tracking)

Email Unsubscribe

Every email has an unsubscribe link. Click it. You'll be removed immediately. No judgment.

Changes to This Policy

If we change how we use data, we'll email you. Big changes get a separate notice.

Questions?

Email us with any privacy questions. We'll answer honestly.
